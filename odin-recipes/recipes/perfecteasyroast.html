The Roast in Question

If you have ever had a dry tough roast, you understand the frustration and desire to find something, any recipe, to satisfy the qualities of a tender, juicy, flavorful roast! Why waste your money on a piece of meat or other form of protein just to consume an unsatisfactory outcome. Among other proteins, I have found that utilizing a smoker to cook roast has been the absolute, most satisfying version.
