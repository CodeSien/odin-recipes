A Lovely Example of the Snow

The problem with shaved ice is the usual under pulverized texture of the solidified water mixing with a sweet liquid applied on top. The flavorless quality of ice reduces the capacity for layering flavor and creating a strong taste on a medium that will be void of sugar juice by the time it melts into water. There is a way to create something like ice cream and shaved ice all-in-one.

Yield 2 Servings

Method of pulverization is best handles with the use of a food processor. It is possible to create the recipe with the use of a cheese grater, but freezing process will vary. For skill level extreme, freeze large block when intending to utilize a cheese grater. For skill level easy, freeze cubes (about 2 trays) to be grated using food processor.

Ingredients

Snow Base

  • creamy beverage or juice of choice [2 cups] frozen

Possible Choices of Toppings

  • fruit
  • condensed milk
  • mochi
  • cereal
  • oreo
